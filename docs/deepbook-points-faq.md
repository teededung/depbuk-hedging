# DeepBook Points FAQ

Source: user-provided notes captured on March 11, 2026.

## FAQ

### What behavior is DeepBook trying to encourage with points?

We want to encourage users to try projects built on DeepBook's engine. We want users to trade.

### Are the current DeFi positions being counted for points or only new ones?

Yes, all the current positions are counted for points.

### How often is the points count updated?

Every week.

### Do I need to hold $DEEP or any other token to earn points?

No.

### Is there a minimum balance required to participate?

No.

### What actions earn DeepBook Margin points?

It's just DeepBook points; it's not only for margin. Using frontends to trade, leverage, swapping through aggregators, deploying capital through Lotus, and so it goes.

### Do I earn points for opening positions, holding positions, or closing positions?

Yes, for all of those.

### Are points earned based on volume, time, or risk?

Based on all of those.

### Do leveraged positions earn more points?

Yes. Duration of the order, and how much interest is paid.

### Do I earn points on both profitable and unprofitable trades?

Yes.

### Are liquidated positions eligible for points?

Yes, if your orders are liquidated, you earn extra points.

### Are there caps on how many points I can earn per day or per account?

No, but points earned will fade the more you earn.

### Do maker and taker trades earn points differently?

Yes, takers earn more points, but we won't say how much more.

### Do limit orders earn points if they don't fill?

No, unless they are margin limit orders, because you paid interest on the borrowed capital while the order was waiting to fill.

### Are there bonus multipliers for early users?

Yes, we give bonus multipliers for Deepest role holders and for Diver role holders. Also, 1M points pass out per week. In the first few weeks, if there are fewer users, then they get a higher amount of points.

### Will the points formula be public?

No. The points formula can change over time. We have the right to deduct points after allocation.

### There will be a public leaderboard?

We likely won't do a leaderboard.

### Are points weighted over time or purely cumulative?

Purely cumulative.

### Which projects have the activities done on them to be counted for the DeepBook points program?

All projects using DeepBook. If they are not on the DeepBook ecosystem page, just ask their team if they are using DeepBook.
